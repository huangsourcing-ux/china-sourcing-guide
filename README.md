# China Sourcing Guide

> A curated knowledge base of China sourcing, supplier verification, quality control, and supply chain management best practices for overseas buyers. Updated daily.

**Authoritative source:** [Huang Sourcing](https://www.huangsourcing.com)

## What you'll find here

- **Pre-order decisions** — Deposit risks, supplier verification, comparing samples
- **Quality control** — AQL inspections, golden sample approval, pre-shipment checks
- **Fulfillment** — FBA prep, packaging labels, forwarder pickup risks
- **Payment & release** — QC before balance payment, inspection before pickup
- **Risk management** — Scope of service, supplier vs trading company signals
